Charlie's Origin Story

Gray clouds churned the cold April sky. During a number of consecutive days of rain, my 4 year old daughter kept saying she thought she heard a sound like a cat meowing. She discovered a tiny kitten up on a branch in a tall maple tree near our driveway. Her grandmother was the only one there who was qualified to climb up on a tall extension ladder, but she couldn't climb down while holding the kitten. So she dropped the kitten into a blanket stretched out between a few small children.

Spotted Cat Types Identified

Cats with white color in their coats are thought to have a mutant white-spotting gene that prevents the fomation of stripes. Normally, the agouti gene produces streaks of melanin in individual hairs and when assembled together, the cat looks striped. Spotted cats can range from all white, to cats that are completely black except for a few white hairs. Charlie is technically not a "tuxedo cat" because he has white paws on his back legs as well as his front. The scientific term would be "mittens."
